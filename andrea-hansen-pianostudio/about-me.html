Get to Know Me

Andrea Hansen, NCTM is a Nationally Certified Piano Teacher with the MTNA (Music Teachers National Association). In 2023 she reached the Permanent Professional status with her Certification. She has taken piano lessons from JeNene Anderson, Doris Adams, Dr. Stephen Allen (BYU-I), & Heidi Leben, NCTM. She has accompanied many choirs, soloists, instrumentalists, and other ensembles. While still in high school she accompanied on the piano the community plays of “Annie” and “Oliver”.

She has been teaching piano lessons for 31 years in Rexburg, Idaho Falls, Blackfoot, & Nampa having a studio with 10-25 students at a time. While in Boise/Nampa area Andrea was very actively involved in the Treasure Valley Music Teachers Association (TVMTA) as a Secretary, Treasurer, Registrar, Newsletter Editor, Sonatina Competition Board Member, Certificate of Merit Chair & Webmaster under 7 different TVMTA Presidents. She helped to design, create & maintain the online judging system that Treasure Valley Music Teachers Association has been using 10+ years for Sonatina Competition as well as Certificate of Achievement and conducted the judges training & onsite tech support for those events.

Andrea is serving as the Registrar for the Idaho Music Teachers Association currently. She is a member of the Eastern Idaho Music Teachers Association, Idaho Music Teachers Association, National Music Teachers Association, and the Bingham County Music Teachers Association.

Andrea returned to the Blackfoot area with her family in 2021 and maintains an active piano studio, runs a bookkeeping business, and stays involved in the lives of her family & friends.
